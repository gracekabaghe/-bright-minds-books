# Privacy Policy for Bright Minds

**Last Updated: November 4, 2024**

## Introduction

Bright Minds ("we", "our", or "us") is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we handle information in our children's reading app.

## Information We Don't Collect

Bright Minds is designed with privacy in mind. We do NOT collect, store, or share:

- Personal information (names, emails, addresses)
- Location data
- Usage analytics or tracking data
- Device information beyond what's necessary for app functionality
- Any information about your child

## Information We Do Collect

The only data processed is:

**Purchase Information** (processed by Google Play)
- When you purchase "Unlock All Books" ($12.99), Google Play handles the transaction
- We receive only a purchase verification token to unlock content
- Payment details (credit cards, etc.) are never sent to us
- All payment data is handled securely by Google

**Local Storage Only**
- Reading progress (which books you've read)
- Achievement badges earned
- App preferences

This data is stored ONLY on your device and is never transmitted to our servers.

## Children's Privacy

Bright Minds is designed for children ages 4-10. We comply with the Children's Online Privacy Protection Act (COPPA):

- We do not collect personal information from children
- We do not allow third-party advertising
- We do not include social features or chat
- We do not share any data with third parties

## Third-Party Services

The app uses:

**Google Play Billing**
- Handles all in-app purchases
- Subject to Google's Privacy Policy: https://policies.google.com/privacy

**PDF Hosting (GitHub Pages)**
- Book content is loaded from GitHub Pages
- No personal data is transmitted
- Standard HTTPS security

## Data Security

- All app data stays on your device
- Purchase verification uses secure Google Play APIs
- No user accounts or passwords required
- No data is transmitted to external servers (except purchase verification)

## Your Rights

You have the right to:
- Delete the app and all local data at any time
- Request purchase refunds through Google Play (within 48 hours)
- Contact us with privacy questions

## Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy occasionally. We will notify users of any material changes through the app or Play Store listing.

## Contact Us

If you have questions about this Privacy Policy, contact us at:

**Email:** brightminds@peguinelibrary.com

## Parent/Guardian Consent

By downloading and using Bright Minds, you (the parent or guardian) consent to this Privacy Policy on behalf of your child.

## Data Deletion

To delete all app data:
1. Uninstall the Bright Minds app from your device
2. All local data (progress, badges) will be permanently deleted
3. Purchases are tied to your Google Play account and can be restored if you reinstall

---

**Summary:**
- We collect NO personal information
- All progress data stays on your device
- Only Google Play processes payments
- Child-safe, privacy-focused design
- No ads, no tracking, no data sharing

Your child's privacy is our top priority.
